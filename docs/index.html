Megan Whetzel

3/07/2021

Introduction

My project will be looking at elk (Cervus elaphus) GPS collar data and hopefully analyzing their movement by utilizing geospatial data such as elevation, habitat type (from NDVI), weather, etc., and comparing it to their locations. I just started my first semester of my Master’s degree in January 2021 and I do not currently have any data available for use from my project.

I hope to use this GPS data from MoveBank (a website for publicly available data) as a surrogate for data I collect in the coming two years. I am unsure of my project yet, but know I will be analyzing ungulate (elk, deer (Odocoileus spp), and livestock) movement through and usage of wildfire-burned areas. The elk data from MoveBank will allow me to look at a similar ungulate species’ movements, as well as prepare me for analyzing my future data; especially if it involves looking at collared wildlife in the state of Utah.
